About us

Paws & Claws Volunteers is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to the welfare and protection of animals. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and homeless animals while promoting responsible pet ownership and animal rights. With a network of passionate volunteers, we offer rescue services, foster care, and adoption programs to ensure every animal finds a loving home.

Our organization conducts various community outreach programs, educational workshops, and fundraising events to raise awareness about animal welfare. We collaborate with local shelters, veterinary clinics, and other animal welfare organizations to maximize our impact. Our team of volunteers is driven by a shared love for animals and a commitment to making a positive difference in their lives.
